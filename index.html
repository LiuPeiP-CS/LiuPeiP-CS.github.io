About Me
Currently, I am an Assistant Researcher at Zhongguancun Laboratory, specializing in the research of code reverse analysis by artificial intelligence. Prior to this role, I earned my Ph.D. from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I focused on open-source cyber threat intelligence(OSCTI) analysis based on natural language processing(NLP) under the esteemed guidance of Professor. Hongsong Zhu. My academic journey further extends to a Master degree, which I obtained through a joint-program between Institute of Computing Technology and Shandong University of Science and Technology. Under the instruction of Professor. Shuqiang Jiang, I explored the realm of multimodal intelligent robots. Before that, I completed my Bachelor degree at Shandong University of Science and Technology, where I got the invaluable guidance and help from Professor. Qingtian Zeng and Peng Zhang.
Research Interest
My research interests mainly include two aspects: natural language processing and cybersecurity. 1) For natural language processing, my research focuses on named entity recognition and relation extraction, as well as multimodal intelligent information processing. 2) For cybersecurity, my research focuses on open-source threat intelligence analysis, reverse engineering, binary code analysis and LLM security.

I am seeking self-motivated Master/Ph.D students to join my team, and I look forward to working with you.
